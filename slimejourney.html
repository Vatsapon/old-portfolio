Slime Journey is a 3D puzzle game where you have to give the Slime a command to take him to the goal. The game concept was inspired by the LightBot and Code.org games. but with additional mechanics.

The player will be given a few commands that have a different kind of action such as Move forward, Turn left, Wait, etc. to put into the Main Function slot which determines the Slime's action. Then, the player can begin the simulation and see the action as it happens in sequence. Players can retry and reorder the commands to see the different results until the Slime can reach the goal/destination.

"Mental Challenge" was the theme assigned to this project. This game is one of the puzzle games that come to mind when someone speaks of mental games.

With that in mind, I created and modified the mechanism to ensure that it worked effectively so as to prepare for future development. Additionally, keep the level and puzzle balanced and challenging to create an "Aha!" moment for those who solve the puzzle.
